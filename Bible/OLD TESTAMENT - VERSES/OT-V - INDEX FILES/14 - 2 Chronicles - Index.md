## Notes

### [[2 Chronicles 20]]
The Lord told them to stay still and not fight this battle. And so, with chaos raging around them, the king called out the band (as happened on the Titanic) and used music to praise the Lord.
There are times in our lives when all we can do is to stand still, and listen to uplifting music that reminds us of the Lord and His mercies, and how He will fight our battles, until things get better.
