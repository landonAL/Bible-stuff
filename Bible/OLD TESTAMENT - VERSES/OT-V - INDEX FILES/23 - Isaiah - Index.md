Chapter 1 - Head and heart: *both* need to be focused on God

>When God is in my head and my heart, my hands will do the right things. My feet will take me to where I need to go. Bryce (Talking Scripture Podcast)

Remnant = survivor

>![[Isaiah 45.7]]

This verse needs a wider context to be understood. I believe that God did not *create* evil, but that He allows it to exist. Evil occurs in the absence of light, of righteousness.
