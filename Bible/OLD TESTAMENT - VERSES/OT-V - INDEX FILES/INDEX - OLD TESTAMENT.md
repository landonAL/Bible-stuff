## Time Periods

1. The Creation and its aftermath
2. Abraham, Genesis
3. Egypt
4. Desert
5. Promised land - tribes
6. Kings - unified kingdom, temple
7. Split kingdoms (Isaiah fits here)
8. Babylonian captivity (exile) 586–520 BC
9. Return to Jerusalem, rebuilding of the temple
10. Dawn of the apocalyptic (visions)
